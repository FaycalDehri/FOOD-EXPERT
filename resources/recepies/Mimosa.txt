INGREDIENTS:

- 6 hard-boiled eggs
- 2 tuna cans
- 3 tablespoons mayonnaise
- 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
- 1 teaspoon vinegar
- Salt and black pepper, to taste
- Paprika or chopped fresh herbs, for garnish (optional)


PREPARATIONS:

1- Cut the hard-boiled eggs in half lengthwise and carefully remove the yolks.

2- Place the egg yolks in a mixing bowl and mash them with a fork or a potato masher until smooth.

3- Add mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, tuna ,white vinegar, salt, and black pepper to the mashed egg yolks. Mix well to combine.

4- Taste and adjust the seasoning as needed.

5- Using a spoon or a piping bag, fill the hollowed egg white halves with the yolk mixture.

6- Optionally, garnish the filled eggs with a sprinkle of paprika or chopped fresh herbs for added flavor and visual appeal.

7- Serve the Hard-Boiled Eggs Mimosa as a delicious appetizer or side dish at room temperature or chilled.
