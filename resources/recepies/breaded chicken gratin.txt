INGREDIENTS:

- 4 Boneless, Fried chicken breasts
- 100 g Flour
- 2 Eggs
 -200 g Breadcrumbs
- 100 g Grated Cheese (such as Gruyere, Swiss, or Cheddar)
- 50 g Butter
- Salt and pepper to taste

3-Dredge each chicken breast in flour, then dip into the beaten eggs, and coat with breadcrumbs, pressing lightly to adhere.

5-In a bowl, mix together the grated cheese, melted butter, and chopped parsley. Spoon the cheese mixture over the chicken breasts in the baking dish.

7-Serve the Breaded Chicken Gratin hot, garnished with additional fresh parsley, if desired. Enjoy!
