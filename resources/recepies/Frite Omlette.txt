INGREDIENTS:

- French Fries.
- 50 ml Milk.
- 2 Eggs.
- Salt & Pepper.

PREPARATIONS:

1- Whisk the eggs with the milk. 

2- Add salt and pepper to the egg mixture (add seasonings to your taste).

3- In a pan, heat up some oil and put your franch fries in it.

4- once the pan hot pour the egg mixture on top of the fries and let it in the pan for a few minutes until the eggs are well cooked. 

5- Serve on a plate and enjoy!
