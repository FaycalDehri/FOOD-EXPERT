INGREDIENTS:

- Pie crust
- Pastry cream
- 500g fresh or frozen cherries
- 100 g granulated sugar
- 50 g cornstarch
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- Whipped cream, for topping (optional)

PREPARATIONS:

1- Prepare the pie crust as per the recipe provided earlier. Roll out the pie crust to fit a 9-inch pie dish, and place it in the pie dish. Trim any excess dough hanging over the edges.

2- Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

3- In a medium saucepan, whisk together the sugar, cornstarch, almond extract, and lemon juice for the cherry filling.

4- Add the pitted cherries to the saucepan and stir gently to coat them with the sugar mixture.

5- Place the saucepan over medium heat and cook, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens and comes to a boil. Continue to stir and cook for 1-2 minutes more.

6- Remove the saucepan from the heat and allow the cherry filling to cool slightly.

7- Pour the cherry filling into the prepared pie crust in the pie dish, spreading it evenly.

8- Bake the pie in the preheated oven for 45-50 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbly.

9- Remove the pie from the oven and allow it to cool to room temperature.

10- Once the pie is fully cooled, you can optionally top it with whipped cream before serving.

11- Serve and enjoy your homemade cherry pie with a luscious cherry filling and a flaky pie crust!
