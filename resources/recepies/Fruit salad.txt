INGREDIENTS:

- Assorted fresh fruits of your choice, such as strawberries, blueberries, raspberries, grapes, pineapple, mango, kiwi, oranges, bananas, etc.
- 2 tablespoons honey
- 1 tablespoon freshly squeezed lemon juice
- Fresh mint leaves, for garnish (optional)


PREPARATIONS:

1- Wash and prepare the fruits by removing any stems, pits, seeds, or peels as needed. Cut larger fruits, such as pineapple, mango, and kiwi, into bite-sized pieces.

2- In a large bowl, combine the prepared fruits.

3- In a small bowl, whisk together the honey and lemon juice until well combined.

4- Drizzle the honey-lemon dressing over the fruits in the bowl and gently toss to coat all the fruits with the dressing.

5- Taste and adjust the sweetness and acidity to your preference by adding more honey or lemon juice as desired.

6- Refrigerate the fruit salad for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together and the fruits to chill.

7- Before serving, garnish the fruit salad with fresh mint leaves, if desired, for an extra burst of freshness and aroma.

8- Serve the fruit salad chilled and enjoy as a refreshing and healthy dessert or side dish.
