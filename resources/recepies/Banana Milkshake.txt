INGREDIENTS:

- 1 Banana
- 200 ml Milk
- 2-3 scoops  Vanilla ice cream
- 1 tablespoon of sugar (optional)
- 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract (optional)


PREPARATIONS:

1-Peel the ripe banana and cut it into chunks.

2-Place the banana chunks, milk, vanilla ice cream, sugar (if using), and vanilla extract (if using) in a blender.

3-Blend on high speed until all the ingredients are well combined and the milkshake is smooth and creamy.

4-Taste the milkshake and adjust the sweetness or flavor with additional sugar or vanilla extract, if desired.

5-Pour the banana milkshake into glasses and serve immediately.

6-Optional: You can garnish the milkshake with a slice of banana or a sprinkle of cinnamon on top for added flavor and presentation.

7-Enjoy your refreshing and delicious banana milkshake!
