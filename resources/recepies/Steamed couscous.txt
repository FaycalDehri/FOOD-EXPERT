INGREDIENTS:

- 1 kg Couscous
- 1 tb Oil
- 50 g Butter
- Salt



PREPARATIONS:

1- In couscous steamer, prepare the couscous according to the package instructions. Usually, it involves adding couscous grains to boiling water, covering the pot, and letting it steam for a few minutes.

2- Take out the couscous and add mix it using ur hands while adding salt and butter

3-Once its all mixed up put it back on the couscous streamer and let it steam until its fluffy and cooked.

4-Serve on a plate
