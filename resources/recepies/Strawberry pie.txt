INGREDIENTS:

- 1 Pie crust
- 200 g Pastry cream
- 750 g fresh strawberries, hulled and halved
- 200 g sugar
- 50g cup cornstarch
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 100 g strawberry jam or preserves
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- Whipped cream, for topping (optional)

PREPARATION:

1- Prepare the pie crust as per the recipe provided earlier. Roll out the pie crust to fit a 9-inch pie dish, and place it in the pie dish. Trim any excess dough hanging over the edges.

2- Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

3- In a medium saucepan, whisk together the sugar, cornstarch, and salt for the strawberry filling. Gradually whisk in 1/2 cup of water until smooth.

4- Place the saucepan over medium heat and cook, whisking constantly, until the mixture thickens and comes to a boil. Continue to whisk and cook for 1-2 minutes more.

5- Remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the strawberry jam or preserves and lemon juice until well combined.

6- Add the halved strawberries to the prepared pie crust in the pie dish.

7- Pour the hot strawberry filling over the halved strawberries in the pie crust, spreading it evenly.

8- Bake the pie in the preheated oven for 45-50 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbly.

9- Remove the pie from the oven and allow it to cool to room temperature.

10- Once the pie is fully cooled, you can optionally top it with whipped cream before serving.

11- Serve and enjoy your homemade strawberry pie with a luscious strawberry filling and a flaky pie crust!
