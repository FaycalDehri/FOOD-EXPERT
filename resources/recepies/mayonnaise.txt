INGREDIENTS:

- 1 egg yolk
- 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard (optional)
- 1 tablespoon vinegar or lemon juice (optional)
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon sugar
- 1 cup vegetable oil


PREPARATIONS:

1- In a mixing bowl, whisk together the egg yolk, mustard, white vinegar or lemon juice, salt, and sugar until smooth.

2- While whisking continuously, slowly pour in the vegetable oil in a thin, steady stream. Make sure to whisk vigorously to emulsify the mixture.

3- Keep whisking until the mayonnaise has thickened and is smooth and glossy.

4- Taste the mayonnaise and adjust the seasoning as needed, adding more salt, sugar, or vinegar to taste.

5- Transfer the mayonnaise to a clean, airtight container and refrigerate until ready to use.
