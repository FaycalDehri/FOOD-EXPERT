INGREDIENTS:

- 225 g boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced
- 225 g fettuccine or other pasta of your choice
- 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 350 ml chicken broth
- 250 ml bechamel
- 50 g grated Parmesan cheese
- Salt and black pepper, to taste
- Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish (optional)


PREPARATIONS:

-Cook the pasta according to package directions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

1- In a large skillet or pan, melt the butter over medium heat.

2- Add the sliced chicken breasts to the skillet and cook until they are cooked through and no longer pink, about 5-7 minutes per side. Remove the cooked chicken from the skillet and set aside.

3- In the same skillet, add minced garlic and cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.

4- Stir in the flour and cook for 1 minute, stirring constantly.

5- Gradually whisk in the chicken broth and bring to a boil. Cook for 2-3 minutes, or until the sauce thickens.


6- Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese until melted.

7- Season the sauce with salt and black pepper to taste.

8- Add the cooked chicken back to the skillet with the sauce and cook for another minute or until the chicken is heated through.

9- Add the cooked pasta to the skillet with the sauce and toss to coat the pasta with the sauce.

10- Serve the white sauce chicken pasta hot, garnished with chopped fresh parsley if desired.
